In 1935, research Programs were introduced. In 1957 the intake of students was increased from 175 to 275 for the College. Pre-professional course was introduced and the entry level was the newly started Pre-University course. This was later changed to a 5 year integrated course.In 1978 Perarignar Anna University of Technology , subsequently the Anna University was established with four constituent colleges, CEG ; MIT ; AC College and S.A.P. The Anna University functioned from the Guindy Campus. Er. P. Sivalingam was the appointed as the first Vice Chancellor. Some other notable Vice Chancellors include Dr. V.C.Kulandaiswamy, Dr M.Anandakrishnan. With profound insight, New branches of Engineering were introduced in the College and a number of UG and PG courses in different disciplines were introduced.
